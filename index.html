ABOUT

Hout Bay Sound is an agile music and events service based in Hout Bay and run by music performer John Bartmann since 2009. John has been the resident events organizer for Tsogo Sun Waterfront Hotel, and has professionally performed at The J&B Met, the Vodacom VJoy Awards and over 100 weddings! John is a vegeterian and neither drinks nor smokes (important around kids). Hout Bay Sound guarantees friendly, clean and affordable sound and music.
